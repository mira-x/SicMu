<i>SicMu player</i> is lightweight audio player which features a single over-all playlist with every song on the device.
Very useful for large music lists organized by folder.

<b>Features</b>

* show songs in a tree view following device's folder hierarchy
* show songs grouping by artist
* show songs grouping by folders
* cover art image
* repeat mode (all, group, one track)
* shuffle mode
* shake the phone to go to next song
* notification with media controls
* disable / enable lockscreen
* advanced playback control (forward/backward of 5/20 s, adjustable playback speed, A to B repeat loop)
* configurable font size
* bluetooth & scrobble support
* play mp3, ogg, flac, midi, wav, 3gp... see android mediaplayer supported media formats (depends on android version).
* light and fast: starts in 0.5s and uses 10Mo of RAM with 5Go of music (1000 files, 100 folders) on a 700MHz ARM processor.
